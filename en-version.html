ABOUT US

Are you curious about lantern art? Have you ever been fascinated by the process of making lanterns? Now, we sincerely invite you to join "E7 Lantern Studio", a creative and open halfway house.

who are we?

We are a group of people who love the art of lanterns, regardless of age, background or experience. We believe that everyone can find their own creative space here.

Our mission:

We are committed to inheriting the essence of Taiwanese lanterns and allowing this traditional craft to shine with new brilliance in modern times. We welcome friends who are employed for the second time, whether you are a retiree or an art lover, Whether you are looking for a new direction in life, you can find your place here.

Our Features:

Learning and communication: Here, you can learn the techniques of making lanterns and exchange experiences with other lantern art enthusiasts. Open Halfway House: Our doors are always open and you are welcome to walk in and share your stories and ideas at any time.
